Privacy Policy

For Duplicate Contact Merge App

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Welcome! Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our Duplicate Contact Merge application ("App") handles your information. The most important thing to know is that we believe your contacts are your private data, and we've designed the App to respect that.

Our Core Privacy Guarantee: Everything Happens On Your Device.

The App performs all its functions—including scanning for duplicates, merging contacts, creating backups, and restoring from backups—entirely on your iPhone or iPad. Your contacts and personal information are never sent to, stored on, or processed by any external servers.

1. Information We Access (With Your Permission)

To perform its functions, our App requires permission to access your contacts. This is the only personal information we interact with. We do not collect, read, or store any other information from your device.

2. How Your Contact Information Is Used

Your contact list is used exclusively for the following features within the App:

  • Merge Duplicates: The App reads your contacts locally to identify potential duplicates and allows you to merge them.
  • Backup Contacts: The App creates a backup file of your contacts. This backup file is stored securely in the App's private local storage on your device, which only you and the App can access.
  • Restore Contacts: The App uses a local backup file you select to restore your contacts.

To be perfectly clear: we do not upload, share, analyze, or even view your contact list. The entire process is automated and confined to your device.

3. Data Storage

All data, including any contact backups created by the App, is stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data. If you delete the App, all associated data, including backups, will be removed from your device.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect your information, we certainly do not share it. The App does not contain any third-party analytics, advertising services, or SDKs that would have access to your contacts.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App's functions are not directed at children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
