Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to SubTrack (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information from users of our SubTrack iOS app (“App”). If you don’t agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

User-Provided Data: When you use SubTrack, you may enter subscription names, amounts, renewal dates, notes, categories, and custom icons. This data is stored locally on your device or synced via iCloud (if you enable iCloud Sync).

Usage & Analytics Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g. number of subscriptions, feature usage) to help improve the App. This information does not identify you personally.

Device & Diagnostics: We may collect error logs, crash reports, and device type (iPhone model, OS version) to diagnose problems and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide subscription tracking, reminders, calendar views, insights, and export functionality.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices via iCloud (if enabled).
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features, stability, and user experience.
  • To diagnose and fix bugs, crashes, or performance issues.
  • To send non-promotional communications (e.g. app updates, maintenance notices) if you allow them.

3. Storage & Security

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device and — if you enable iCloud Sync — in your iCloud account. All data transmissions (syncing) are encrypted in transit. We use industry-standard security practices to help protect your data.

We do not store your data on our own servers (beyond aggregated analytics), and we do not share personally identifiable subscription data with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Third-Party Services & Integrations

SubTrack may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting (e.g. Apple’s App Analytics, Crashlytics). These services operate under their own privacy terms. We do not allow third parties to collect subscription content or identifiable user data.

5. iCloud Sync & Apple Privacy

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in Apple’s iCloud infrastructure and managed under Apple’s privacy terms and encryption. We do not access your iCloud data. iCloud usage is entirely optional.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete subscriptions or clear all data from within the App at any time. Once deleted, we cannot recover your data. If you stop using the App or delete it, your data will no longer be retained by us.

7. Legal Disclaimers & Limitations

The App is provided “as is” without warranties. We disclaim responsibility for direct or indirect losses arising from your use of SubTrack, including financial decisions based on the App’s data. You are responsible for managing your subscriptions and finances.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy over time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-App notification or App Store update. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
