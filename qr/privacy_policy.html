Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to QR Code Generator – Scanx (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our iOS app (“App”). By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

QR Code Generator – Scanx is designed to be privacy-first and fully offline. The App does not collect or transmit your QR code data to external servers.

User-Provided Data:

  • QR content you scan or create (URLs, text, WiFi details, contacts, emails, events, phone numbers, SMS, locations)
  • Custom designs, templates, logos, colors, titles, and notes
  • Your scanning history, favorites, and folder organization

No Tracking or Analytics:

The App includes no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking tools, or third-party data collectors. We do not build usage profiles or track your behavior.

Diagnostics (Optional):

If you opt-in to share diagnostics with Apple, anonymized crash reports may be provided to us. These reports never contain QR content or personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information available on your device only to:

  • Enable scanning, generating, customizing, and exporting QR codes
  • Store your history, templates, and settings locally on your device
  • Improve performance through anonymous crash diagnostics provided by Apple

3. Storage & Security

All QR code data, customizations, history, and settings remain stored locally on your device. We do not operate backend servers and cannot access your data.

Deleting the App will remove all stored data. If your device is backed up with iCloud or your computer, the App’s data may be included in those backups under Apple’s control.

4. Third-Party Services

QR Code Generator – Scanx does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Any content you share through iOS (such as using the system share sheet) is processed by that third-party app under its own privacy policy.

5. iCloud & Backups

The App does not directly sync data to iCloud. However, your device may include App data inside standard iCloud or encrypted local backups if you have enabled them. We do not have access to these backups.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete any QR code, template, or history item at any time. To remove all data, simply delete the App — iOS will erase all associated local storage.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age required by your local laws (e.g., 13 or 16). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The updated version will always be available on this page.
