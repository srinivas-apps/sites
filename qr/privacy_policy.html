Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

ScanX (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the ScanX iOS app (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

ScanX is built to operate privately and fully offline.

User Data Stored Locally:

  • QR content you scan or create (URL, text, WiFi, contacts, emails, events, SMS, location)
  • Custom designs, templates, logos, titles, colors and patterns
  • Your scan history, favorites, folders and lists

No Tracking or Analytics:

ScanX does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking libraries or data collection tools.

Crash Reports (Optional):

If you opt-in to share diagnostics with Apple, Apple may provide anonymized crash logs. These contain no personal data and no QR content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide all core features (scan, generate, customize and export QR codes)
  • To store your templates, history and settings locally on device
  • To improve stability using anonymous crash diagnostics from Apple

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers and do not have access to your data. Deleting the app removes all stored data. Device-level backups (iCloud or computer) may include App data depending on your system settings.

4. Third-Party Services

ScanX does not use third-party analytics, advertising or tracking tools. Any content shared through iOS (e.g., Messages, Mail) is handled by those apps under their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

ScanX is not directed to children under the age of digital consent in your region (e.g., 13 or 16). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be available on this page. Continued use of the App means you accept any updated terms.
